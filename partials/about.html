What is RateMyClass?

RateMyClass, developed by a group of Information School students at the University of Washington, is your ultimate information source for reading reviews about certain courses at your university! RateMyClass was developed by students, for students, and is maintained by students to ensure our service remains as accurate as possible.

You can be certain that reviews about each class provided through our service are legitimate and written by actual students who have taken the course. Our patented verification system ensures that every single review is a correct direct representation of the student's overall perception about the course. No instructor has access to leave a review about a course, it is entirely up to the students to review classes they have taken.
